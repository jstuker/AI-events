A hands-on workshop for educators exploring how to integrate AI tools into teaching.

## Workshop modules

- AI-assisted lesson planning and curriculum design
- Automated assessment: possibilities and ethical boundaries
- Personalized learning paths with adaptive AI
- Hands-on: Building a simple AI tutor with no-code tools
- Group discussion: AI policy in Swiss education

All participants receive a toolkit of recommended AI resources for educators.

The annual gathering of Switzerland's natural language processing community.

This two-day conference brings together researchers and practitioners from across Switzerland and Europe to share the latest advances in NLP, from large language models to multilingual processing.

## Highlights

- Keynote speakers from leading European AI labs
- Paper presentations and poster sessions
- Industry demo track
- Swiss multilingual NLP challenge results
- Doctoral consortium for PhD students

## Bern AI Ethics Roundtable

An invitation to think critically about AI in the public sector. This roundtable brings together diverse voices to discuss fairness, accountability, and transparency in government AI systems.

### Discussion Topics

- Algorithmic decision-making in social services
- Transparency requirements for public AI systems
- Citizen rights and AI-powered automation
- Lessons from the EU AI Act for Swiss governance

### Format

- 10:00 — Welcome coffee
- 10:30 — Opening remarks by Prof. Dr. Claudia Wenger
- 11:00 — Guided roundtable discussion
- 12:30 — Summary and next steps
- 13:00 — Light lunch

Limited to 30 participants. Registration required.

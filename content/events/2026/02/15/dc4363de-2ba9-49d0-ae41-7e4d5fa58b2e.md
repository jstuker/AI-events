## Zürich Swiss AI Research Day

The premier event for Switzerland's AI research community. Hear about groundbreaking work from the nation's top labs and connect with fellow researchers.

### Programme

- 09:00 — Welcome by Prof. Dr. Andreas Krause, ETH AI Center
- 09:30 — Keynote: "Foundation Models for Science" — Prof. Ce Zhang, ETH Zürich
- 10:30 — Coffee break
- 11:00 — Research spotlight talks (15 min each)
- 12:30 — Lunch and poster session
- 14:00 — Panel: "Funding AI Research in Switzerland"
- 15:00 — Lab presentations: ETH, EPFL, IDSIA, UniBas, UZH
- 17:00 — Best paper award ceremony
- 17:30 — Networking apéro
- 18:00 — End

### Call for Posters

Submit your poster abstract by February 1, 2026. Open to all Swiss AI researchers.

Free registration. Online attendance available via livestream.

## Basel AI Art Exhibition

Where does artificial intelligence end and art begin? This special evening exhibition at HeK brings together works that challenge our understanding of creativity in the age of AI.

### Featured Works

- **"Latent Landscapes"** — AI-generated Swiss mountain panoramas by Claudia Meier
- **"The Uncanny Brush"** — Interactive painting installation by collective404
- **"Data Portraits"** — Visualizing personal data as abstract art by Yann Brodeur
- **"Echoes"** — AI-composed soundscape responding to visitor movement

### Programme

- 17:00 — Doors open, guided tours available
- 18:30 — Artist talk: "Co-creation with Machines"
- 19:30 — Live performance: AI-human music collaboration
- 20:30 — Drinks at the museum bar

Reduced entry (CHF 10) for students with valid ID.

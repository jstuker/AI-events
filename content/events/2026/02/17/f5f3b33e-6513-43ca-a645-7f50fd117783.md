## Lucerne AI and Education Forum

How should Swiss schools and universities adapt to the AI revolution? This forum brings together educators, technologists, and policymakers to chart a path forward.

### Sessions

- 09:30 — Opening: "AI Literacy as a Core Competency" — Rektor Prof. Dr. Kathrin Krammer
- 10:15 — "ChatGPT in the Classroom: One Year Later" — Panel with teachers
- 11:15 — Coffee break
- 11:45 — "Personalised Learning with AI Tutors" — Dr. Simon Pfister, Stiftung Mercator
- 12:30 — Lunch
- 13:30 — Workshops (choose one):
  - A: AI tools for lesson planning
  - B: Assessment in the age of AI
  - C: Teaching AI ethics to students
- 15:30 — Closing panel: "Policy Recommendations for Swiss Education"
- 16:30 — End

Lunch and materials included in the registration fee.

## Lausanne AI in Finance Summit

The Swiss Finance Institute presents its annual summit on artificial intelligence in financial services. Learn from practitioners how AI is reshaping banking, insurance, and asset management in Switzerland.

### Keynotes

- "AI-Driven Portfolio Optimization" — Dr. Christine Moser, Head of Quant Research, Credit Suisse
- "Fraud Detection at Scale with Graph Neural Networks" — Amit Sharma, VP Engineering, SIX Group

### Sessions

- 09:00 — Registration
- 09:30 — Opening keynote
- 10:30 — Track A: Trading & Markets / Track B: Risk & Compliance
- 12:30 — Networking lunch
- 14:00 — Track A: RegTech & Compliance / Track B: InsurTech
- 16:00 — Panel: The Future of AI in Swiss Banking
- 17:00 — Closing remarks and apéro

Premium catering and networking opportunities included.

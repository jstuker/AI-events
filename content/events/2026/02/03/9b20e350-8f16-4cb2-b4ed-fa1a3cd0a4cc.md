## Basel Robotics and AI Meetup

Where robots meet intelligence! Join the Basel Robotics Community for an evening of talks, live demos, and hands-on interaction with autonomous systems.

### Programme

- 18:30 — Doors open and networking
- 19:00 — "Reinforcement Learning for Dexterous Manipulation" — Dr. Felix Brunner
- 19:30 — Live demo: Autonomous navigation in cluttered environments
- 20:00 — "Sim-to-Real Transfer: Bridging the Gap" — Lena Hofmann
- 20:30 — Open discussion and demos
- 21:00 — End

Drinks and snacks provided. All levels welcome!

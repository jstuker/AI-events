## Geneva AI Startups Pitch Night

Ten hand-picked Swiss AI startups take the stage to present their innovations to investors, corporate partners, and the tech community. Who will win the audience vote?

### Format

- 18:00 — Doors open and welcome drinks
- 18:30 — Startup pitches (5 minutes each + 3 min Q&A)
- 20:00 — Audience voting
- 20:15 — Winner announcement
- 20:30 — Networking apéro with investors and founders

### Jury

- Marie-Claire Dupont, Partner at Lakestar
- David Frey, Managing Director at Swisscom Ventures
- Prof. Aisha Khan, EPFL Innovation Park

Free entry. Registration required for capacity planning.

A 48-hour hackathon combining AI and robotics — build AI-powered robotic prototypes with real hardware.

## Challenges

1. **Pharma Lab Assistant** — Build an AI-guided robotic arm for lab sample handling
2. **Warehouse Navigator** — Create an autonomous navigation system for logistics robots
3. **Open Track** — Bring your own idea using provided hardware kits

## What's provided

- Robotic arm kits, sensors, and microcontrollers
- GPU-equipped workstations
- Mentorship from industry experts
- Meals and snacks for the full 48 hours
- CHF 10,000 prize pool

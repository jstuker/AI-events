Leading Swiss banking and fintech executives come together for a moderated panel discussion on the transformative impact of AI on financial services.

## Panelists

- Head of AI, UBS Innovation Lab
- CTO, Swiss National Bank (Digital Currency Division)
- CEO, a Basel-based AI fintech startup
- Professor of Computational Finance, University of Basel

## Topics

- Algorithmic trading and risk management with AI
- Regulatory frameworks for AI in Swiss banking
- AI-driven fraud detection and compliance
- The future of personalized banking

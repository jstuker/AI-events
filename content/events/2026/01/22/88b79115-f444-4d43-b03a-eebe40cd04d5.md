## Zürich LLM Applications Panel

How are Swiss companies deploying large language models in production? Join us at Technopark for an expert panel discussion on the practical challenges and solutions for LLM-powered applications.

### Panelists

- Sarah Keller, CTO at SwissNLP AG
- Dr. Thomas Weber, Head of AI at UBS
- Maria Santos, ML Platform Lead at SIX Group
- Moderated by Dr. Lukas Bauer, Swiss AI Association

### Topics

- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) in enterprise settings
- Fine-tuning vs. prompt engineering trade-offs
- Cost management and latency optimization
- Data privacy and compliance in Switzerland

Doors open at 18:00. Drinks and snacks provided.

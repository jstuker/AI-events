## Geneva Machine Learning Workshop

A comprehensive, hands-on workshop designed for professionals looking to deepen their machine learning skills. Hosted at Campus Biotech in the heart of Geneva's innovation district.

### What You'll Learn

- Supervised and unsupervised learning techniques
- Feature engineering and model selection
- Evaluation metrics and cross-validation
- Deploying models to production

### Requirements

- Laptop with Python 3.10+ installed
- Basic programming experience
- Familiarity with statistics is helpful but not required

Lunch and refreshments are included in the ticket price.

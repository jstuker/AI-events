## Lausanne Deep Learning Seminar

The EPFL AI Center invites you to an afternoon of deep learning research presentations and discussions. This free seminar features talks from EPFL faculty and visiting researchers.

### Programme

- 14:00 — Welcome and introduction
- 14:15 — "Scaling Laws and Efficient Training" — Prof. Léa Dubois
- 15:00 — "Multimodal Foundation Models" — Dr. Yuki Tanaka
- 15:45 — Coffee break
- 16:15 — "Attention Beyond Transformers" — Prof. Raj Patel
- 17:00 — Panel discussion
- 17:45 — Networking apéro

Registration is free but required due to limited seating.

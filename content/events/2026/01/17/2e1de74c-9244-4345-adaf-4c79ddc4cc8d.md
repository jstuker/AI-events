## Basel Computer Vision Symposium

Join leading researchers at the University of Basel for a day of cutting-edge presentations and discussions on computer vision. This hybrid event is accessible both in person and online.

### Keynote Speakers

- Prof. Dr. Anna Müller — "Diffusion Models for Medical Imaging"
- Dr. Marco Rossi — "Real-time Object Detection at Scale"

### Agenda

- 10:00 — Registration and coffee
- 10:30 — Keynote sessions
- 12:30 — Lunch break
- 13:30 — Paper presentations
- 16:00 — Poster session
- 17:30 — Closing remarks

Online participants receive the livestream link 24 hours before the event.

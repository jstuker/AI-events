## Bern NLP Fundamentals Bootcamp

Dive deep into natural language processing with this intensive one-day bootcamp at the University of Bern. Led by researchers from the Bern AI Lab, you will gain practical skills in modern NLP techniques.

### Topics

- Text preprocessing and tokenization
- Word embeddings (Word2Vec, GloVe, FastText)
- Transformer architecture deep dive
- Building NLP pipelines with Hugging Face
- Fine-tuning pre-trained language models

### Prerequisites

- Python proficiency
- Basic understanding of neural networks

Lunch and materials included.

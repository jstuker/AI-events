## Zürich AI Kickoff Meetup

Kick off the AI year with the Zürich community! This informal meetup brings together professionals and hobbyists to share insights on the latest developments in artificial intelligence.

### Programme

- 18:00 — Doors open & networking
- 18:30 — Lightning talks (5 min each)
- 19:30 — Open discussion: AI trends for 2026
- 20:30 — Drinks and networking

All skill levels are welcome. Bring your curiosity and business cards!

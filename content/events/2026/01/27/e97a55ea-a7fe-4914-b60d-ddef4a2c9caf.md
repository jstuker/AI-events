## St. Gallen AI for Business Workshop

The HSG Institute of AI in Management presents a hands-on afternoon workshop designed for business professionals who want to understand and leverage AI without writing code.

### Workshop Modules

1. **AI Landscape Overview** — Understanding what AI can and cannot do today
2. **Use Case Identification** — Finding high-impact AI opportunities in your organization
3. **Tool Demo** — Hands-on exploration of AI-powered business tools
4. **Implementation Roadmap** — Creating a practical AI adoption plan

### Who Should Attend

- C-suite executives and department heads
- Project managers and business analysts
- Entrepreneurs exploring AI integration

No technical background required. Bring your laptop.

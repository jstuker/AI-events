## Zürich Generative AI Hackathon

Build something amazing with generative AI in just one day! Hosted at Google Zürich, this hackathon brings together creative minds to push the boundaries of what's possible with generative models.

### Challenge Tracks

1. **Productivity** — Tools that enhance workplace efficiency
2. **Creative** — Art, music, or storytelling applications
3. **Social Good** — Solutions addressing societal challenges

### Prizes

- 1st Place: CHF 2,500
- 2nd Place: CHF 1,500
- 3rd Place: CHF 1,000

### Schedule

- 09:00 — Breakfast and team formation
- 10:00 — Hacking begins
- 17:00 — Code freeze
- 17:30 — Presentations
- 19:00 — Awards ceremony
- 19:30 — Pizza and networking

Teams of 2-4 people. Solo participants can form teams on site.

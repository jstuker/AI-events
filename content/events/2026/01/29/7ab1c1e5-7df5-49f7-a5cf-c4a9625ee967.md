## Geneva Responsible AI Conference

Geneva's tradition of international governance meets the challenge of AI ethics. This conference brings together global voices to discuss frameworks for responsible AI development and deployment.

### Tracks

- **Governance**: EU AI Act implementation, Swiss regulatory landscape
- **Fairness**: Bias detection and mitigation in production systems
- **Transparency**: Explainable AI and model interpretability
- **Sustainability**: Environmental impact of large-scale AI training

### Featured Sessions

- Opening keynote by the UN Special Envoy on Technology
- Fireside chat: Swiss Federal Council perspective on AI regulation
- Workshop: Conducting AI impact assessments

Simultaneous interpretation available in English and French.
